Latitude vs. Max Temperature

This graphs indicates that the hottest temperatures are in cities located within 20 degrees of the Equator. The majority of the cities in the sample appear to be found north of the Equator. Furthermore, the graph suggests that the farther from the Equator the city is situated, the lower the maximum temperature.

Finally, the graph shows that there is a strong correlation in this sample between latitude and maximum temperature.
